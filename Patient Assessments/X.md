Special cases:
- Is it a Torso puncture?
	- Apply **chest seal** occlusive dressing
	- *Chest seals are vented, preventing tension pneumothorax*
- Is bleeding Arterial and on limb? (Spurting)
	- Apply **Tourniquet** proximally
		- Write down time on tourniquet

Use lease invasive method of stopping bleeding:
- Does **Point Pressure** work?
	- Apply Israeli Band-Aid
	- If no Israelis are available, applying a dressing, put an item on it, wrap around item against the wound 
- Does holding down a dressing on it stop the bleeding?
	- Apply a 4x4 or abdo-pad, wrap it up
- If none of the above works, apply **Tourniquet** on limb
	- Write down time of application on tourniquet
